Pumpkin Pancake

Description

This recipe is easy to make. Super delicious~. For those of you who enjoy sticky rice food receipe. This is a must-have.

You can make as many as you like in one shot. Store them in the freezer for whenever you feel the crave for them. Just fry them on low-heat setting. :D

 

Ingredients: (Check the box to show you've got the ingredient~)

Pumpkin puree: 600g
Glutinous rice flour/Sticky rice powder: 360g
Sugar: 50g

Directions: (Click on the instruction text to display or hide photo)

  1. Add sugar to the pumpkin puree as you like. Stir until sugar dissolves.

  2. Add sticky rice to the sweetened puree. Remember to add only a little amount each time, and stir. Repeat until the paste become unsticky.

  3. Separate the paste into chunks, and rub the pieces to the shape of pancakes with diamter of a baby's palm with 1 cm in depth.

  4. Add oil into your frying pan. Fry the pumpkin pancake with low-heat. Cook until the surface become golden and crispy. You can store the rest of the pancake paste in your freezer.
